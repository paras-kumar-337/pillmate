### PillMate - Smart Medicine Reminder System

# Problem Statement:
Many patients forget to take their medications on time,
leading to several health issues, especially elderly people.
A mobile application is needed that can remind the users
to take their medications at the scheduled time.

# Objectives:
1. To allow users to add their medications with
necessary details such as name, dosage, time etc.
2. To notify users at the correct time with
sound/notification/vibration.
3. To track and maintain the history and log of
medications taken.
